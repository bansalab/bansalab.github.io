Profile

Electromagnetics researcher and antenna engineer specialising in beam-steering millimetre-wave antennas, metasurfaces and reconfigurable intelligent surfaces, and bespoke 3D-printed dielectric lenses for 5G/6G, satellite and sensing systems. Combines research-grade depth with industrial delivery experience from the Satellite Applications Catapult, and translates electromagnetic theory into measured, manufacturable hardware. Author of 60+ peer-reviewed publications; recipient of the IEEE Antennas and Propagation Society Young Scientist Award (2025).

Research Interests

Beam-steering mmWave antennas · Metasurfaces & reconfigurable intelligent surfaces (RIS) · 3D-printed graded-index, homogeneous and Luneburg dielectric lenses · Orbital angular momentum (OAM) · Optically transparent antennas · Integrated sensing and communications (ISAC) · Satellite and non-terrestrial networks · Leaky-wave antennas and substrate-integrated waveguides.
